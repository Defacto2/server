**tPORt** or _the Power Of Reversing team_ were a cracking and programming group from the 2000s likely based in or coordinated from Russia. The group was founded by both Cerber and The DarkStranger, and the two of them would often take turns at running the group.

They would regularly publish membership information and a hall of fame, which was unusual in a time when most groups had gone anonymous to avoid profiling by law enforcement.

tPORt Hall of Fame: Bizon, Ev1l^4, Cerber, Crel, Fau$t, Helios, M!h@n, Paxan, R3r, Red Bar0n, The DarkStranger.
