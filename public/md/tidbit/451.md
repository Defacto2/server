**Lightforce** is a storied group having first been founded as _The LightForce (TLF)_ on the Commodore 64 in September 1986 by Boomer, Master of Chaos, and Zar out of West Germany. In early 1988, they also opened operations on the Commodore Amiga, and like many successful groups on that system, eventually worked with video game consoles and portable game platforms.

_Faster than light since 1987_

In 2000, they were advertising for suppliers on the Gameboy Color, N64, and PSX. But 15 years later they'd be all in on the 3DS, Vita, and later the PS4, and eventually the Switch.

But in a novel retro-throwback, during September 2000, they [released](/f/a922b19) the ISO to the 1994 game Alien Breed Tower Assault, for the commercially unsuccessful Commodore CD32.
