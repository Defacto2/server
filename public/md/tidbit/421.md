**Pussycat** was a prolific console European release group from the 2010s, which generally focused on the popular handheld devices of the day, including the DS, 3DS, Switch, the PSP, Vita, and also disk images for the Wii and Wii U.

Our collection includes over a thousand releases that are referred to as "proofs". A term used to describe an included photograph to prove the legitimacy of the Scene release. Usually, these include a single photo with the game cartridge, or game disc, the retail box, and a handwritten note showing the group name. The idea was to prove the game was sourced from retail media, and not a pre-release copy given to media or testers.

The group would also occasionally release some unpublished games, such as 2006's [unreleased](/f/b42df7f) Isle of Minno (c) SCEE.
