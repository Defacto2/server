**CyberCrime Inc** or CyberCrime International Network is a free global BBS network that describes themselves as "the ultimate, coexisting union of all three phases of the computer underground - piracy, artwork, hack, phreak." The network only permitted a maximum of five boards per area code or country code, and offered the following message boards:
