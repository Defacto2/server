**The Crazed Asylum**, or TCA, was a notorious Scene website created and maintained by Black Acid, which ran from 1997 until 1999. Black Acid would regularly update his site with both gossip, online chat logs, and drama on the Scene at large, often with crude commentary and jokes as one might expect from a late teenager who was online in the 1990s.

In an era that was a decade before social media, one element certain Sceners found captivating was the web-based message forum that TCA hosted, think 4chan but for software piracy. Where registered users could post private and public messages to each other, much like Sceners did on electronic bulletin boards 5-10 years earlier. But instead of reaching a localized audience of BBS callers, the forums were open to anyone worldwide with Internet access, a web browser, and a registered account on TCA.

Something that is lost today is how expensive hosting content online used to be in the 1990s. A .com domain, for example, would be $100 USD for a minimum two-year contract, and that is mid-1990s money, and decent web hosting could cost that amount per quarter. So it is possible Black Acid was a bit of a web geek and self-hosted his webpage from his home.
