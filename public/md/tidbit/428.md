**BEAT** or TEAM BEAT was a niche release group that released music-creation synthesiser software and other tools.

They celebrated their,

- 100th [release](/f/b42aeec) in March 2004.
- 200th [release](/f/b828277) in September.
- 400th [release](/f/b61c4d7) in May 2005.
- 600th [release](/f/b419c6b) in April 2006.
- 700th [release](/f/b32a28a) in September.
