**PWA**

The origin of PWA is odd and goes back to June 1991, when a Michigan bulletin board system operator by the name of Dr.Modem teams up with a fellow system operator Dumper to [release](/f/b62cb55) Ultima Martian Dreams under the convoluted brand Electronic's Boutique Cracking Group.

Then, in October 1991, we see the game release group PWA. Comprising Dr.Modem, a few system operators from Minneapolis, San Fernando Valley, and importantly Dumper with his [board](https://demozoo.org/bbs/3747/) Dumper's Den. PWA may have only lasted a month or two, and it seems the name had no meaning, but I like to imagine it was a nod to a corny pro-wrestling association. Afterwards, in 1992 Dumper and Dumper's Den would find themselves as a part of the International Network of Crackers or [INC](/g/international-network-of-crackers).
