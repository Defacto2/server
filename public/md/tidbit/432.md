Our **Venom** (V!) collection is incomplete, but the brand started releasing game titles for the GBA. A handheld first sold in the USA and Europe during mid-2001 by a famously litigious company. However, we only have two releases from this era, one that [claims](/f/) Rocky from Ubisoft is the first GBA game with copy-protection.

_It may come as a surprise to some, but what we have here is the first copy-protected Gxxxbxx Advance game. Don't worry tho, it seems the VENOM dreamteam knocked Rocky's protection out in round three._

The bulk of the collection comes from the decades later, still for US and European video-game consoles, but releases for the 3DS, PSP, Vita, Wii U, and finally the Switch. The group would tag these later releases with a one-liner suggesting they were the same people who were releasing at the start of the millennium. And occasionally, they would [promote](/f/a51c064) software that they thought was cool.

_VENOM - The Playmakers! Since 2001._
