**Fighting for Fun** or FFF was a self-described web-cracking group based in France. Founded in late March 2002 with numerous releases immediately following, FFF saw great success operating within its own online bubble, cracking software sourced from online registrations and distribution, and rejecting the traditional Scene, its sourcing of boxed retail titles, and combining cracks with the software for redistribution.

<q>FFF does not make and will never make warez.</q><br>
<small>[https://defacto2.net/f/b11fab5](/f/b11fab5)</small>

One counterargument against this form of prolific cracking was the obsessive-compulsive behavior of these crackers to release every minor point release or update, creating a flood of cracks for near-identical software. For example, hypothetical cracks of mIRC v4.00, v4.01, v4.02, v4.02b, v4.03, ... are all counted as separate releases.
