**The Sabotage Rebellion Hackers** or TSRh were a cracking group founded by B-$hep and Oxen [possibly](/f/ad1f0b5) in August 2001. However, a [drama text](/f/b324103) from 2009 wrote they are from Russia and were founded in 1999 originally as the _web_ cracking group The Sabotage Rebellion.

The _web_ cracking meant that instead of uploading their cracks to Scene FTP sites, they'd post them to forums hosted on websites, and possibly even their own group webpage. These websites and forums acted as a parallel cracking Scene that had little to do with the traditional so-called "elite" Scene.
