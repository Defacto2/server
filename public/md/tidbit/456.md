There were at least two groups that used the brand, Orion. The [first](/f/b022cfb) was a cracking group that operated in 1998.

The second and more successful **Orion** was an application, utility cracking, and occasional game release group founded on the 3rd of February 1999 by tirre and sketch.

In mid-2000 they [wrote](/f/a73138), _Things have been going great in the year 2000 for ORiON! We have become the most successful cracking group this year and continue to be the top releasing cracking group in the scene._

Originally group had a hubristic [attitude](/g/orion), _ORiON is proud to announce that it was the #1 group over the entire year of 2000 having more releases than any other group... We are now one of the best cracking groups of all time, and continue to be the top releasing cracking group in the scene._

However, [later](/f/b12d2ce) milestones saw a toned down ego, _Today we celebrate ORiON 5th Birthday (Feb 3rd) we are very happy to still be here after 5 years. In our 5 years here our team is proud to have brought to you over 9000+ releases_

In May 2004, they [celebrated](/f/a920497) 10,000 releases.
