**MAGE** or Master Artists Guild for the Elite was a vaporware group that saw two major announcements penned by Groo, 
the first in March 1992, which was published in the [APEX newsletter](/f/a81bb9e), and the second in May, which was published in [The Product](/f/a6513).

---

> M A G E :  1st. Public Announcement
>
> MAGE (Master Artist Guild for the Elite) is a new group formed to pay tribute to the best art and artists
> the pirate community. MAGE is not a competitive art group. It's sole purpose is to honor and offer more
> visibility to the finest artwork in the Pirate Community.
>
> MAGE shall be the first collection to offer a dependable showcase of the finest artwork (from all art groups as well as independents).

---

> MAGE Announcement #2
>
> MAGE periodically puts together some of the best known, finest, Pirate Art available.
> These samples are placed into a Voting Program \[Thank you, Force Ten\] and voted on by the MAGE Council, and the pirate public at large.
>
> When all votes have been duly tabulated, the results will appear in a prestigious **MAGEzine**.
