**Reaction** and **Prestige Console** were both console release groups that, in 1994, focused on the SNES. 
In January 1995, a [release](/f/a91e0cd) announced that they had teamed up to form the new brand named **Cream**. 
<em>CREAM is a joint venture of REACTION and PRESTIGE. We merged knowing each other's powers and weak points...
The main leaders in CREAM are Deathknight and The Unknown Gamer.</em>
