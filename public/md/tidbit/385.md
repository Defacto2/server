In the March 1991 release of [The Basket Manager](/f/a51fe1c) by **The Cracking Lords**, the group consists of three members, who declare, "The First Cracker's Elite in Italy" and "The first Italian MsDos cracker group".

An early April 1992 [release](/f/a21b3d1) by **Dead Memory** proclaimed, 
"The Cracking Lords and Enigma now together in a new, cool association: DEATH MEMORY".
